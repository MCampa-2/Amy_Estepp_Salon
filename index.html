Hello and Welcome

My name is Amy Estepp. I have been a hairdresser for 20 years with experience in hairdressing, highlighting, color, and haircutting. Throughout my 20 years of doing hair, I have attended several advanced training courses to perfect my techniques. I have trained at Vidal Sassoon, Wella Color Studio, Oribe Studios, and at Bumble and Bumble University. I have trained with Nick Arrojo, The Doves, Oribe, and several other very talented Hairdressers. Two years ago, I took an advanced test with the State to be considered a (Master)Senior Stylist. I plan to continue learning and attending classes to advance my skills. I take my career very seriously and want to make sure I am very knowledgeable to always give my clients the very best. I worked at a very upscale Salon in Montgomery County for 18 years and recently decided to branch out on my own and created A Stepp Above Salon Studio. I am located inside Sola Salons, in Studio #10. I love meeting new people and look forward to watching my new space grow. When I am not hairdressing, I enjoy spending time with my family.
